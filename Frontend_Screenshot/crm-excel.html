How it works
What happens to your data on each run
Date normalisation
Excel serial dates → DD/MM/YYYY. All date columns auto-detected and converted.
CTR calculation
Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100. Clamped to global CTR range and deduped against yesterday.
Video metrics
VCR = Completions ÷ Starts × 100. Kept within campaign or global 75–89% range.
Viewability
Viewable ÷ Measurable × 100. Campaign-specific or global 75–89% fallback applied.
Add Campaign Rule
Set custom CTR, VCR and Viewability ranges per campaign. Leave a range empty to fall back to the global default. Click "+ Add More" to add multiple rules at once.
